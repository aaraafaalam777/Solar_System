Neptune


Neptune is the eighth planet from the Sun and the farthest IAU-recognized planet in the Solar System(earlier Pluto). It's the densest giant planet.Being composed primarily of gases and liquids, it has no well-defined solid surface.

Pluto is a dwarf planet in the Kuiper belt, a ring of bodies beyond the orbit of Neptune. It is the ninth-largest and tenth-most-massive known object to directly orbit the Sun.
